Signature Veg Biryani

A fragrant masterpiece. Slowly cooked layers of spices, basmati rice, and farm-fresh vegetables.

The Royal Aroma

Our Vegetarian Biryani is legendary. It challenges the conventional and delivers a spectacular punch of perfectly balanced spices. We use the finest long-grain Basmati rice, premium saffron, and an aged blend of whole spices.

Cooked using the traditional 'Dum' method, the biryani seals in the aromas, allowing the rice and vegetables to steam together perfectly. It is the heart of any feast and a guaranteed crowd pleaser.

Every order of Biryani is accompanied by our traditional cooling Onion Raitha and hearty Brinjal Curry (Kathirikai Gotsu) to complete the experience!
